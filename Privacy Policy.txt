We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Bubble Chess (the "App") collects, uses, and safeguards information. By using the App, you agree to the terms of this policy.

2. Data Collection and Processing
The App operates on a "Local-First" architecture.

A. Personal Data
The App fetches public chess game data (PGN files) which may include:

Usernames (e.g., from Lichess.org or Chess.com).

Game moves and timestamps.

We do not collect, transmit, or store this data on our own servers. All data fetching is performed directly from your device to the third-party providers. The data resides solely in your device's local memory or storage.

B. Device Permissions
To function, the App requires the following permissions:

Internet Access: To connect specifically to Chess.com and Lichess.org APIs.

Storage (Read/Write): To save and retrieve PGN files on your device (if applicable based on Android version).

3. Third-Party Services and APIs
The App acts as a client interface for third-party services. When you use the "Import" feature, your device connects directly to:

Chess.com: Subject to their Privacy Policy.

Lichess.org: Subject to their Privacy Policy.

We do not act as a middleman for these connections. Your IP address and request headers (including the App's User-Agent) are visible to these services during the connection.
